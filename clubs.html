The Art Club

The Art Club focuses on the expression and the qualitative preoccupation of its members, with various creative activities, promoting, thus, the expression and creativity, and the enhancement of imagination. Nevertheless, our club combines painting with hand makings, expeditions & exhibitions, and also meetings with artists. At the workshops of the club, one can discover hidden talents of oneself, and also escape from the daily routine, as a Techie here!




The Japanese Club

The Japanese Language Club holds and trains the students who have n interest over Japanese Language and the Japanese Culture, regardless of their skill level. The mission of JLC is to provide a flexible outlet for students to share their interests in topics related to the country Japan, and also be preparing for the Japanese Language Proficieny Test (JLPT). クラブへようこそ !




The Entrepreneurs' Club

The Entrepreneurs' Club is a learning-by-doing environment where students can cultivate their entrepreneurship skills. The club is also a place where people in the community can incubate a business idea, providing students with learning opportunities as the students help develop the idea through the club.




The Coding Club

Here, in The Coding Club of our Department, students and programmers are authorized to compete to crack Hackathons, solve coding problems, take part in Discussion Forums etc., This club is open to any programming language and just involves problem-solving and thus, sharpening Coding Skills!




The Sport Club

The Sport Club is committed to provide a healthy sporting habit among the students. It helps to learn teamwork at work, coordination among diverse cultural & ethnic groups and mainly infuses discipline & instills the value system in one individual. Value of time, precision & competitiveness are the major learning points apart from communication, coordination & teamwork. Gear Up!
